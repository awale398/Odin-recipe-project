Mahamri na Chai is a beloved coastal Kenyan breakfast combo. Mahamri are sweet, spiced, deep-fried dough pieces made with coconut milk and cardamom, while chai is Kenyan spiced tea brewed with milk, sugar, and aromatic spices. Together, they make a warm, comforting, and flavorful morning treat.

  2. Add Wet Ingredients
    • Add coconut milk and margarine/oil. Mix and begin kneading, adding warm water gradually to form a soft, elastic dough.
  3. Knead the Dough
    • Knead for 10‚Äì15 minutes until smooth. Cover and let it rise in a warm place for 1‚Äì2 hours until doubled in size.

    • Heat oil in a deep pan. Once hot, fry the mahamri in batches until golden brown on both sides.
